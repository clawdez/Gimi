# Partnership and acquisition scope

This document separates product assets from private commercial terms. It does
not state a price, grant access, transfer IP, or make an offer.

## Potential acquisition assets

- Gimi source code and agreed commit history.
- Solana program source and generated IDL.
- Product design, brand assets, demos, and documentation owned by the seller.
- Deployment configuration templates without credentials.
- Defined transition support and knowledge transfer.
- Domains or social assets only when explicitly listed in a signed schedule.

Third-party services, accounts, API keys, user data, licenses, and partner data
are excluded unless ownership and transfer rights are independently verified.

## Preferred funded partnership

JhiNResH remains involved as product and technical lead across:

- product direction and architecture;
- agent workflow and approval design;
- operator discovery and pilot execution;
- security and production-readiness decisions;
- roadmap, hiring, and team formation.

The partner should contribute more than capital: a bounded operator venue,
inventory, invited users, distribution, compliance ownership, or engineering
resources.

## Terms requiring explicit agreement

- funding and milestone schedule;
- IP ownership or license scope;
- founder role, decision rights, time commitment, and equity/vesting;
- exclusivity, geography, and pilot field;
- warranties and liability;
- transition support;
- follow-on and acquisition options;
- termination and IP reversion;
- treatment of data, credentials, and third-party accounts.

## Diligence boundary

Do not disclose production credentials, unredacted operational data, private
price terms, or unrestricted repository access before identity, authority,
purpose, and disclosure scope are verified.
